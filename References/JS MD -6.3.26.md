## Key Findings
- **The Strategy & Operations co-primary lane is by far the deepest live market.** Google (multiple YouTube S&O Manager roles), Stripe (Connect, Enterprise, Partnerships), Notion (Partner, Central GTM, Customer Success), Amazon (Creator Services), Databricks, and Snowflake all have live Manager/Senior-Manager S&O postings in NYC or Remote-US that map almost exactly to the candidate's OKR/annual-planning, executive-presentation, partner-strategy, and SQL-modeling strengths.
- **The Applied-AI Product lane is thinner at the "business-workflow / decision-support / AI-adoption" altitude the candidate prefers.** Most AI-lab PM roles (Anthropic, OpenAI, Microsoft AI) are consumer/technical PM or developer-platform PM. The closest matches are AI-enablement / AI-adoption / AI-outcomes roles (OpenAI Product Enablement, Glean AI Outcomes Manager) and AI-driven operations roles (Databricks Strategy & Execution Manager, PS).
- **Compensation generally clears the bar.** NYC big-tech bands routinely top out $210K–$330K. Where pay isn't posted (OpenAI, Snowflake, several Google reqs), it should not be a screen-out — flag as Unknown and recruiter-ping. Aggregated total-comp at the frontier labs is very high: Anthropic PM total compensation in the U.S. ranges from $468K to $651K per year (median ~$467,670 reported on Levels.fyi, updated May 12, 2026).
- **Retail/commerce-tech AI roles exist and pay well** (Etsy ML Enablement PM, $194K–$252K) but are engineering-led platform roles, so they rank as secondary per the candidate's stated preference.
