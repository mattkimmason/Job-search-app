# Application Packet Template

Use one copy of this template per high-priority role.

## Packet Workflow (Use in Order)

1. Confirm role score and lane in `job-search/tracker.md`.
2. Select resume track:
   - Choose `AI` when JD emphasizes AI product, AI adoption, transformation, internal tools, or applied AI workflows.
   - Choose `Retail Systems` when JD emphasizes merchandising systems, inventory/planning, Anaplan/SAP, integrations, or operational retail tech.
3. Tailor the top section of the resume:
   - Reorder the top 5 bullets to mirror JD priorities.
   - Keep quantified impact bullets near the top.
   - Align wording to role keywords without overstating scope.
4. Draft outreach:
   - Build one recruiter message using JD language and your strongest matching outcomes.
   - Build one hiring manager note focused on role scope and cross-functional delivery fit.
5. Build interview hooks:
   - Prepare 3-5 stories that map to role requirements (ownership, transformation, technical-business translation, adoption outcomes).
6. Execute and log:
   - Submit application.
   - Send outreach within 24 hours.
   - Update tracker status and dates.
